# Consumer Mapping Question Sequence

Use these 7 questions to build a Hell/Heaven Island mapping from a funnel or landing page.

## The Questions

1. **What's going on in their minds at this stage?** (mental state when they land)

2. **What do they want permission to believe?** (permission beliefs)

3. **What imagery catches their attention?** (visual world)

4. **What phrases catch their attention?** (language/vocabulary)

5. **Describe their life recently — what's been happening acutely that brought them here?** (Hell Island scenes)

6. **What's Heaven Island imagery they imagine subconsciously?** (the transformation they're buying)

7. **What are symptomatic moments around the most critical times where they feel most insecure?** (specific Hell Island texture — before/during/after the critical moment)

## Output Format

Save to `[client-folder]/[product]-consumer-mapping.md`:

```markdown
# [Product] — Consumer Hell/Heaven Island Mapping

## Hell Island (The Problem)

### The Problem Itself
[Specific texture — not just "it sucks" but the scenes, silences, internal scorecards]

### The Silence/Aftermath
[What happens after the failure moment]

### The Private Search
[How they've tried to solve it alone]

### The Avoidance
[How they've started steering away from situations]

### Failed Attempts Track Record
[What they've tried that didn't work — CRITICAL for copy]

### The Slow Erosion
[What's quietly slipping away beyond the surface problem]

## Heaven Island (The Transformation)
[Specific scenes of life after the product works — not abstract, visual and concrete]

## Copy Ammunition

### Explicit Problem Phrases
[Exact phrases that name the problem — "finishing in under 2 minutes", not "the thing he struggles with"]

### Specific Transformation Phrases
[Exact outcomes — "lasted so long I asked if he took something", not "she noticed a difference"]

### Mechanism Explanation
[Why does it work? "trains the muscle that controls when you finish"]

### Failed Attempts to Reference
[Pills, creams, breathing tricks, whatever they've tried]
```
